Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 18, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Athkar app. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our app.

ðŸ”’ We do not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties.

Data We Collect

Athkar app collects the following data locally on your device only:

  • Location Data: Used only to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction. Your location is not stored or sent to any server.
  • Settings and Preferences: Such as selected Adhan sound and font size are saved locally on your device only.
  • Favorite Athkar: Saved locally on your device.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes only:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographical location
  • Determine Qibla direction from your current location
  • Save your preferences to improve user experience
  • Send prayer and Adhan notifications (if you allow it)

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. All data remains on your device only.

Required Permissions

  • Location Permission: To calculate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • Notification Permission: To send prayer and Adhan alerts

You can deny these permissions, but some features may not work properly.

Data Security

Since all data is saved locally on your device, its security depends on your device security. We recommend:

  • Using a strong screen lock
  • Keeping your operating system updated
  • Not installing apps from untrusted sources

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
  • Deny any permissions the app requests
  • Clear app data from your device settings

Children

Athkar app is suitable for all ages. We do not collect any personal data from any user regardless of their age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us
